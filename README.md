# 🪶 Uncaged Quill

> *Write what you mean. Say what you need.*

A lightweight, fully offline tool that helps writers identify flagged or sensitive language in their documents and find alternative phrasing — so your ideas survive the gatekeeping without losing their meaning.

---

## Who is this for?

Researchers, grant applicants, and academic writers who navigate institutional language guidelines and want to revise strategically — without compromising the core of what they're saying.

---

## What it does

- 📄 **Upload any `.docx` file** — your document never leaves your device
- 🔍 **Scans for flagged words or phrases** from a customizable list
- 💡 **Suggests synonyms and alternatives** so you can rephrase with intention
- ✅ **Accept or flag each change** inline — green for accepted, orange for manual review
- 📥 **Download the revised document** with all original formatting preserved

---

## Why offline?

Because the content you're revising is often sensitive, institutional, or unpublished. No API calls. No data sent to any server. Everything runs locally in your browser.

---

## How to use

1. Download `uncaged-quill.html`
2. Open it in any modern browser — no installation needed
3. Upload your `.docx` file
4. Paste or load your list of flagged terms
5. Review suggestions inline and download your revised document

---

## Features

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| File support | `.docx` |
| Synonym source | Datamuse API (online) with offline fallback |
| Data privacy | 100% local — nothing leaves your device |
| Dependencies | None to install — runs in the browser |
| Formatting | Fully preserved on download |
